Welcome to the AFM Compliance Advisor user guide. This feature ensures all mortgage advice meets Dutch AFM (Financial Supervision Act) requirements under Wft regulations.

üéØ Overview

The AFM Compliance Advisor validates mortgage advice against Dutch regulatory requirements, ensuring compliance with:

  • Wft Article 86f - Client suitability assessment
  • BGfo Article 8.1 - Mandatory advisor remuneration disclosures
  • BGfo Article 9.1 - Risk warnings and consumer protection
  • Documentation requirements - Complete audit trail
